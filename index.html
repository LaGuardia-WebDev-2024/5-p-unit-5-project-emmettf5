Why YOU should support your local music scene

You should support your local music scene because the shows are cheaper than any other, and your tickets help keep the music coming and thriving. Only YOU can prevent empty concert venues.

Barbes

576 9th St

Awesome music here that is incredibly unique and contemporary

Terraza 7

40-19 Gleane St

Awesome jam sessions and live music with lots of roots in latin rhythms

Gold Sounds Bar

44 Wilson Ave

Crazy venue that hosts many different hardcore bands, certain to have a crazy experience when coming here.

These places stay alive by people showing up and listening to these great mucisians, and many of these places are struggling to keep the music coming. Your support means you not only get to listen to incredible music, new and old, you also get to support local venues to keep bringing great shows to the people of the community.
